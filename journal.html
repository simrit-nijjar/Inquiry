How did I decide my theme?
  • I chose my theme after the recent laws that have been proposed in multiple areas of the world recently. Events such as the passing of Article 13 and the destruction of net neutrality had been on mind for a while, so that aided in my inspiration to use this theme. I used these influences and thought about a broader picture, which to me was How much of ourselves do we still own?.
How did I choose my area?
  • My entire project is pretty set on being based on the web and online activities, so the clear choice to present these findings was New Media.
How did I choose the form to express my area?
  • I chose to use a blog in particular because I have some experience in certain aspects of web creation and design, so a blog/site seemed to be the best way to utilize my limited skill.
How did I address difficulties?
  • Anytime I had a problem like, for example, when I had a problem creating my site, I slowed down and backed up from what I’m trying to do. I would than slowly go through the problem step by step until I found the specific part or location of the problem, and then correct it.
What did I learn through the process?
  • During this project, I learned the difficulties of just searching for information and copying down what I saw, because that wasn’t enough for what I was trying to showcase. I had to dive deeper into actual articles of individual events that I was trying to research, like the references of wikipedia pages and the articles that they linked to.
What did I accomplish?
  • I accomplished quite a bit during this project. For starters, I was able to research something I chose by myself to finish a project I created for myself. This is something new to me, as I would normally just be given a topic for a certain project when required to do so. Another thing I accomplished was the creation of fully functioning site with a purpose. I have practiced creating sites in the past, but this is the first time I’ve created a site with a purpose from the ground up (mostly).
Where will this work be heading in grade 11?
  • I don’t know, but I hope to continue with New Media for the rest of my highschool years enthusiastically. I’m not sure about continuing this theme I have created for myself, however, as I’m not so sure I liked to research it as much as I could with other topics.
Any final thoughts?
  • I would just like to state that did enjoy this project very much, though it was very tough to grasp the concept during the beginning phases, and it still is every now and then. I would definitely be content with having to carry out this type of research project in the future and get the opportunity to do a much better job than I have currently have, not that I think I’ve done a bad job but just that I think I could have done better.
