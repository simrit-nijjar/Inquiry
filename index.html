MY INQUIRY PROJECT

ABOUT

How much of ourselves do we really own?

This project is all about the idea of how much of ourselves we truly own. During the reearch phase, I went through three different companies: Google, Microsoft, and Apple, to see how their security and past exploits could be seen in today's light. In this site, you will find different types of blog posts representing goals that each go through the different types of things that would matter when choosing what information to give to a company. It involves data breaches, privacy terms, and a short summary for those who do not want to read all the way through.
