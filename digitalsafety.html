Goal 2: HOW SAFE IS MY INFO WITH THESE COMPANIES?

Google:

Cyber Attacks:
  • Google has three major data breaches in the last decade. One occured in 2014 on their email application Gmail with over 5,000,000 accounts being compromised via a hack.
  • The second occurred in early 2018 on the social platform Google+ , with over 500,000 accounts being compromised because of poor security.
  • The latest one occurred late 2018 again on Google+, with 52.5 million account left vulnerable to developers because of poor security.
Breached Data:
  • Names
  • Email Addresses
  • Occupations
  • Genders
  • Ages

Microsoft:

Cyber Attacks:
  • In 2001, Microsoft becomes the prominent victim of a new type of hack that attacks the domain name server. In these denial-of-service attacks, the DNS paths that take users to Microsoft websites are corrupted.
  • On february 8th of 2012, electronic contract company Foxconn was hacked by Swagg Security . This hack released Microsoft’s bank credentials.
Breached Data:
  • Employee Bank Credentials
  • Redirected Users To Phishing Sites

Apple:

Cyber Attacks:
  • On february 8th of 2012, electronic contract company Foxconn was hacked by Swagg Security . This hack released Apple’s bank credentials.
  • In 2013, Apple’s developer hub was hacked which exposed 275,000 names, mailing addresses, and/or email addresses.
Breached Data:
  • Employee Bank Credentials
  • Names
  • Mailing Address
  • Email Address
